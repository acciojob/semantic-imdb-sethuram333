Summary

Inception is a science fiction movie where skilled thief Dom Cobb enters people's dreams to steal secrets. He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can perform the impossible task called "inception" — planting an idea into someone's subconscious. The movie explores layers of dreams, reality, and the power of the mind.

Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, and Ellen Page (now Elliot Page) as Ariadne. Their performances make the movie intense and memorable.
